About AI Search Visibility

What is AI search visibility?

AI search visibility measures how well your website appears in AI-powered search results from ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and other generative engines. It combines traditional SEO with AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) and GEO (Generative Engine Optimization).

How is this different from regular SEO?

Traditional SEO optimizes for search engine ranking algorithms that return blue links. AI search visibility optimizes for generative AI models that synthesize answers from multiple sources. A high AI visibility score means your content is structured so AI models can find, extract, and cite your information when generating responses.

Do I still need traditional SEO?

Yes. SEO brings traffic from traditional search engines. AEO captures featured snippets and voice search. GEO ensures your brand appears in AI-generated answers. All three work together — our audit scores you across each dimension so you know where to focus.

About Rank GAS

How does the free audit work?

Enter your website URL on our homepage. Rank GAS scans your site for AI readiness across 5 key dimensions: entity signals, passage-level structure, answer-first content, citation potential, and crawler access. You receive a visibility score plus a prioritized checklist of missing elements.

About AI Citations

How do I get cited by ChatGPT?

ChatGPT and similar models cite sources based on authority, originality, and extractability. Focus on: publishing original research and data, maintaining consistent entity signals across the web, structuring content in independent passage-level sections, and ensuring your site is accessible to AI crawlers like GPTBot.

Can I check if I'm already being cited?

Our Growth and Agency plans include citation monitoring. We track brand mentions across major AI platforms and alert you when your content appears in AI-generated responses.
