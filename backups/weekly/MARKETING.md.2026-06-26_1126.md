## Facebook Ad Creative (Primary Channel)

### Why Facebook over Google
SME owners scrolling Facebook don't know they're eligible for grants. Facebook creates demand.
Google captures intent. Facebook creates it — at 4-8x lower cost per click.

**Body:** Singapore alone has S$15B+ in allocated SME grants sitting unclaimed. You're probably eligible for some of it. Free scan — 60 seconds. No obligation.

## Facebook Audiences

### Audience 1 — SG SME Owners (Broad)
- Location: Singapore
- Age: 25-65
- Job titles: Business Owner, Founder, Director, Managing Director, CEO, SME Owner
- Interests: Enterprise Singapore, SME support, business grants, small business
- Estimated reach: 180K-250K

### Audience 2 — Industry Laser (by Country)
- Location: Target country
- Job titles: Owner, Director + Industry-specific
- For SG: add interests "Food manufacturing", "Retail", "Logistics", "F&B"
- For MY: add "Halal", "Manufacturing", "Palm oil", "Tech startup"
- For PH: add "Agriculture", "BPO", "Manufacturing"
- Estimated reach per country: 50K-120K

### Audience 3 — Lookalike (after first 500 scans)
- 1-3% lookalike of people who completed free scan
- Exclude existing scan completions

### Budget Recommendation
- Start: S$15-25/day per country (= S$450-750/month for SG only)
- Scale winning ads after 2 weeks
- Retargeting: S$5-10/day
