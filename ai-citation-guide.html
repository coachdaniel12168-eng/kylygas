How to Get Your Brand Cited by AI Search Engines

When someone asks ChatGPT "what's the best CRM for small business," the AI draws from its training data and crawl data. The brands that get cited built strong entity signals and structured data that AI models trust.

6 Factors That Determine AI Citation

  1. Organization Schema with sameAs: The single strongest signal. Tells AI "this is a real company." Without it, your brand is anonymous to AI models.
  2. FAQPage & HowTo Schema: When AI answers "how" or "what" questions, it looks for pages with FAQPage or HowTo markup. These pages get excerpted 3x more often than unmarked pages.
  3. Content Depth & Structure: AI extracts meaning from heading hierarchy and list structure. Poorly structured pages produce garbled AI excerpts — so AI skips them.
  4. External Citation Patterns: Sites linking to authoritative sources signal participation in a legitimate information ecosystem. AI weights this as a trust signal.
  5. Author Attribution: Content with clear author bylines (name + bio) gets cited more than anonymous content. AI uses authorship as a credibility proxy.
  6. Freshness Signals: Content updated this month is more likely to be cited than content from 2023. AI tracks modification dates.

The Competitor Gap

Most companies score below 50% on their first AI visibility audit. The biggest gaps are almost always the same three: no JSON-LD, no sameAs links, and poor heading structure. All fixable in under an hour.
