Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 June 2026

1. What We Collect

When you use GovFund's free funding scan, we collect:

  • Company name
  • Industry/sector
  • Country of operation
  • Business details relevant to funding eligibility (employee count, revenue range, years in business, etc.)
  • Email address (optional — only if you want follow-up)

We do NOT collect: passwords, bank details, SingPass/CorpPass credentials, identification numbers, or credit card information through the free scan.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide your free funding eligibility results
  • To follow up with relevant funding opportunities (only if you provide your email)
  • To improve our matching algorithms and database accuracy
  • To compile anonymous aggregate statistics

3. We Never Sell Your Data

GovFund does not sell, rent, or trade your personal or business information to third parties. Ever.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers with encryption at rest and in transit. We maintain hourly backups with 24-hour retention and daily backups with 30-day retention.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@scalesmes.com.

6. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for functionality (country selection, form state). We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

7. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Payment processing (Airwallex — governed by their privacy policy)
  • Email delivery (Resend — governed by their privacy policy)
  • Website hosting (GitHub Pages — governed by their privacy policy)

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for businesses and business owners. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18 years of age.

9. Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of material changes to this policy via our website. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions: privacy@scalesmes.com
