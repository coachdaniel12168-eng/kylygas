How to Rank in AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the process of structuring your content so that answer engines — like Google's featured snippets, voice assistants (Siri, Alexa), and AI chatbots — can extract and deliver direct answers from your pages.

What Is an Answer Engine?

An answer engine doesn't just return links — it returns the answer itself. When you ask Google "how to improve AI search visibility," the featured snippet at the top is AEO at work. When Alexa reads out a recipe, that's AEO.

5 AEO Best Practices

  1. Answer-First Structure: Place the direct answer in the first paragraph, then elaborate
  2. Question-Based Headings: Use H2/H3 tags phrased as questions people actually ask
  3. Structured Data Markup: FAQPage, HowTo, and Q&A schema help answer engines parse your content
  4. Concise Paragraphs: Keep paragraphs under 3 sentences — ideal for voice and snippet extraction
  5. List & Table Formats: Numbered lists and comparison tables are heavily favored for snippets

AEO vs GEO — What's the Difference?

AEO targets structured answer delivery (snippets, voice). GEO targets generative AI citation (ChatGPT, Perplexity). They overlap but require different optimization approaches. AEO needs schema markup and answer-first writing. GEO needs original data and citation signals.
